Retirement Portfolio Withdrawal Projection

Key Assumptions

  • Planning Horizon: Projection runs until the later of your or your spouse's life expectancy
  • Stock Withdrawal Strategy: Fixed first-year amount, adjusted annually for inflation
  • Additional Income Growth: Increases based on inflation
  • Social Security Benefits: Entered in today's dollars they are adjusted for inflation
  • Death of Spouse: Surviving spouse receives the max SSN benefit of either spouse
